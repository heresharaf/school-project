Birthplace of Buddha

Latest Updates on Lumbini

UN Secretary-General António Guterres visited Lumbini in October 2023, emphasizing global peace and Buddhism's teachings.

On December 12, 2023, Nepal's Prime Minister unveiled a conservation plan for the Ramagrama Stupa, the only undisturbed stupa with Buddha's relics.

Key Attractions

  • Maya Devi Temple: The sacred birthplace of Buddha, with archaeological remains dating back to the 6th century BCE.
  • World Peace Pagoda: A white stupa built by Japanese Buddhists, symbolizing peace.
  • Ramagrama Stupa: An untouched ancient stupa believed to contain Buddha’s relics.
